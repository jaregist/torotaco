Our Vegetables

We source all of our vegetables from local farmers in North Carolina. We have partnered with several farms that specialize in vegetable farming rather than row crops to make sure ingredients like our tomatoes, onions, and potatoes are delicious, healthy, and stimulate the local economy.

All vegetables are grown organically without the use of pesticides or herbicides. The farmers are skilled in no-till farming and natural techniques to help ward off insects, weed, and disease. This allows us to provide a product untainted by chemicals.

We visit each local farm several times throughout the year to verify the quality and quantity of product. This allows us to guarantee a fresh, steady supply of local vegetables year-round.
