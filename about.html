Our Team

Toro Taco is proud to be a living wage employer. We think that our team will be more focused, attentive, and caring if they don't have to worry about making ends meet outside of the "office". We have a relatively small crew but, we are a passionate bunch. On a daily basis you will likely see our two owners, head chef, sus chef, and wait-staff working hard to bring you the best tacos around!

Our Story

Toro Taco opened in 2020 when we saw the need to bring authentic Mexican cuisine to the Triangle in a sit-down restaurant environment. We wanted to offer the freshest ingredients with recipes that have been passed down from our families for generations. We are located in a historic warehouse building in downtown Durham, North Carolina and are open 7 days a week.
