Our Meats & Cheeses

We source all of our meats and cheeses from local farmers in North Carolina. We have partnered with several farms that specialize in ethical livestock farming to provide these ingredients for us. Working with these farmers encourages ethical animal treatment while helping the local economy.

Every animal product (meat, cheese, eggs, etc) that is served at Toro Taco comes from hormone-free, free-range animals. While this causes a minimal increase in price, we feel that this extra cost is worth it because, the animals are treated better and are generally healthier than livestock from factory farms.

We visit each farm several times per year to verify the quality and quantity of product. This allows us to guarantee a fresh, steady supply of meats and cheeses while also ensuring that the animals are being cared for to our high standard.
