Hand-Made Tortillas

Our tortillas are the best in town! We make a fresh batch of them every morning with the best ingredients sourced directly from Mexico. We serve corn tortillas primarily but, make a portion on wheat tortillas for those who prefer them.

Authentic Menu

We have worked hard to bring our customers a menu of authentic dishes. Our menu isn't as large or varied as your typical Tex-Mex restaurant but, we specialize in meat, salsa, and tortilla recipes that have been passed down through the generations to give you a real taste of Mexico.

Outdoor Patio

Our beautiful outdoor patio is first-come, first-serve seating. We have dedicated wait-staff for this area so you don't have to worry about being forgotten about. Enjoy the same delicious menu and bar selections out here on those nice North Carolina days.

Premium Bar Selection

Our bar carries a wide variety of different alcoholic (and non-alcoholic) beverages. We offer a large selection of tequilas, mezcals, and Mexican lagers as well as plenty of domestic beers and liquors.
